Adding to 30 Game

Rules: Two players take turns adding a number from 1 to 5 to the current total. The player who makes a sum of 30 wins.

For example, the first player can say "0+1=1", "0+2=2", "0+3=3", "0+4=4", or "0+5=5".

If the first player says "0+3=3", then the second player can say "3+1=4", "3+2=5", or anything up to "3+5=8".
